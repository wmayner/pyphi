Added the cascade tie-resolution primitive at ``pyphi.resolve_ties``. The cascade walks the IIT postulate hierarchy (Existence → Intrinsicality → Information → Integration → Exclusion → Composition, plus a pyphi-specific Determinism canonicalization level) and resolves ties at the lowest sufficient postulate. ``ResolutionContext`` carries the entry-point function's escalation budget (so a low-level tie does not trigger expensive higher-level recomputation when the consumer would compute the same value anyway) and memoizes per-candidate intermediate values across cascade calls. ``CascadeLevel`` describes one step in a cascade; ``CascadeOutcome`` is the structured result, surfacing the tied set and the resolution level for diagnostics. ``NotAComplex`` is raised when a cascade reaches its final level with a tie under ``on_unresolved='fail'``.
