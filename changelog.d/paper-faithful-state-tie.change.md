When a substrate's cause and effect specifications tie at maximum intrinsic information, ``Substrate.sia()`` selects the joint ``(cause_spec, effect_spec)`` pair whose system phi ``φ_s = min(φ_c, φ_e)`` is greatest, per Albantakis et al. 2023 S1 Text. The selection is exposed via :func:`pyphi.resolve_ties.resolve_state_tie`. The full tied set is preserved on ``SystemIrreducibilityAnalysis.system_state.cause.ties`` / ``.effect.ties`` and on the SIA's ``.ties`` (per-pair SIAs surfaced for inspection). Substrates with no state ties bypass the cascade.
