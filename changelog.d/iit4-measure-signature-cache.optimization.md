The measure-shape classifiers in ``pyphi.measures.protocols`` (``satisfies_composite_measure`` and siblings) are now memoized per measure function. They introspect a measure's signature via ``inspect.signature``, which is immutable for a given registered measure, but were re-deriving it on every mechanism/system partition; caching collapses this to one introspection per measure (e.g. ~1000 redundant ``inspect.signature`` rebuilds eliminated per cause-effect structure on a 3-node system).
