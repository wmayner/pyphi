``Substrate`` and ``System`` now render rich display cards instead of one-line summaries. The ``Substrate`` card shows a labeled connectivity grid (rows = from-unit, columns = to-unit) and embeds the TPM's state-by-node grid; the ``System`` card surfaces the per-unit state (e.g. ``A=1, B=0, C=0``), the candidate units, the background units fixed by conditioning, the applied cut (if any), the system's connectivity grid, and its effective cause- and effect-side TPMs (the background-conditioned marginals over the system's units, toned cause/effect) — which for a subset system genuinely differ from the full substrate's TPM. The one-line forms are retained as the low-verbosity / embedded representation. The IIT 3.0 and 4.0 SIA cards now display the MIP as an embedded cut-matrix grid in addition to the concise partition headline, and the ``CauseEffectStructure``/``PhiFold`` cards embed a relations table (relata, ``φ_r``, degree) alongside the distinctions table. In HTML output, symbol subscripts such as ``φ_s``, ``φ_d``, and ``Σφ_r`` now render as real ``<sub>`` subscripts (terminal/ASCII output keeps the underscore form).
