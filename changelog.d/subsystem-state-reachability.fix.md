``validate.state_reachable`` now restores the subsystem-level reachability check that was lost during the ``Subsystem`` → ``System`` rename. In addition to the substrate-level joint-probability check, the validator now requires the subsystem's component of the state to be in the image of the background-conditioned subsystem dynamics. Systems whose ``proper_state`` is unreachable under the conditioned effect TPM now raise ``StateUnreachableForwardsError`` at construction, and ``substrate.possible_complexes`` filters them out as documented. This restores the pre-2.0 behavior under which single-node subsystems whose conditioned dynamics couldn't produce their state were skipped during ``all_sias`` iteration.
