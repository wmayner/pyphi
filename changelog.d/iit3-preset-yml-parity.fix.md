``presets.iit3`` now matches ``pyphi_config_3.0.yml`` exhaustively. Previously ``purview_tie_resolution`` was ``"PHI"`` rather than the YAML's ``["PHI", "PURVIEW_SIZE"]``, ``assume_partitions_cannot_create_new_concepts`` was implicit (default ``False`` happened to match), and ``actual_causation.alpha_measure="PMI"`` was implicit. Substrates with phi-ties at the purview level (``rule110`` at state ``(1,0,1)``, ``grid3`` at state ``(1,0,0)``) will see a slightly different ``sia.phi`` under the preset now that the secondary ``PURVIEW_SIZE`` key breaks ties; tie-free substrates (``basic``, ``xor``) are unaffected. ``IITConfig.purview_tie_resolution`` is now annotated ``str | list[str]`` to match the underlying ``resolve_ties.purviews`` contract.
