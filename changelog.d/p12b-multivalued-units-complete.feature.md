``pyphi`` now supports multi-valued (k>2) units throughout the SIA / CES pipeline. Substrates with heterogeneous per-node alphabets are constructed via ``Substrate(state_space=...)``; ``FactoredTPM`` is the canonical representation for both cause and effect TPMs (unified per IIT 4.0 Eq. 4). Two new k>2 golden fixtures (``multivalued_k3_tiny``, ``multivalued_2x3x3``) verify end-to-end correctness. The 23 binary goldens remain byte-identical. The ``actual_causation`` (AC) pipeline remains binary-only; AC k-ary support is deferred.
