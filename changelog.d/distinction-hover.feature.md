In the simplicial-complex view (`pyphi.visualize.plot_ces`), hovering a distinction
now shows richer detail. A purview dot names its state-cased purview and side, that
side's φ, and the parent distinction (mechanism, φ_d, and the opposite purview); a
mechanism label names the mechanism, φ_d, both purviews with each side's φ, and the
total relation φ (Σφ_r) the distinction carries.
