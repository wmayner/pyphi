Added the CES-completeness confirmation experiment (`test/test_ces_completeness.py`,
ROADMAP P11.95c case b). Brute force on small binary substrates shows the
cause-effect structure is **not** a complete invariant of the substrate in
general — non-isomorphic substrates can share an identical CES (down to
repertoires and relations under relabeling) whenever a unit is left
unconstrained by the structure. Restricted to irreducible systems (the
complexes a CES is properly the structure of), no counterexample is found, so
the incompleteness is confined to reducible systems. This settles the open
theory question gating P11.95c case (b).
