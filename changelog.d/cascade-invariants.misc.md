Added ``test/test_cascade_invariants.py`` — Hypothesis-driven property tests for the cascade primitive. Covers: (1) the resolved winner is always in the input candidate list; (2) the tied set is always a subset of the input; (3) the resolved winner attains the max key at each cascade level; (4) input ordering does not affect the resolved winner; (5) budget blocks escalation past ``max_escalation_level``; (6) full budget escalates to ``Composition``; (7) ``on_unresolved="fail"`` raises ``NotAComplex``; (8) when the cascade resolves at level K, all survivors entering K were tied at all earlier levels (monotonicity); (9) ``resolve_complex_tie`` resolves on a unique-big_phi winner. Plus a 50-iteration sequential and parallel stress test on the AND-XOR substrate (the canonical state-tie fixture) and a sequential-equals-parallel determinism check.
