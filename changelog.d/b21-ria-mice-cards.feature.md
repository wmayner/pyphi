The ``RepertoireIrreducibilityAnalysis`` and MICE cards are reorganized into labeled sections grouped by concern — a summary, the **MIP** (partition), the **Ties** counts (state/partition, plus purview ties for MICE), and the **Repertoire**. The repertoire is now shown as a styled distribution table (rows = purview states, columns ``Pr`` and ``Pr (cut)``, tied states marked) instead of preformatted ASCII; a scalar repertoire still shows the forward/partitioned probabilities. A new verbosity level ``FULL`` (``config.infrastructure.repr_verbosity = 3``) adds the partition's cut-matrix grid to these cards (above the default ``HIGH`` = 2).
