Display verbosity (``config.infrastructure.repr_verbosity``) now gates the rich cards consistently across the terminal and HTML backends: ``LOW`` (0) shows the one-line compact form (a leaf in HTML), ``MEDIUM`` (1) shows the card without the expensive embedded TPM grids, and ``HIGH`` (2, the default) shows everything. The embedded TPM grids — a ``Substrate``'s TPM and a ``System``'s background-conditioned cause/effect marginals — are computed only at ``HIGH``, so a ``System``'s marginals are no longer computed when displaying it at a lower verbosity.
