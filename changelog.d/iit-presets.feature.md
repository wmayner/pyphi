Added canonical IIT settings presets `iit3_settings`, `iit4_2023_settings`, and `iit4_2026_settings` (exported from `pyphi` and `pyphi.conf`) for one-call formalism switching via `with config.override(**iit3_settings): ...`. Each preset configures the IIT sub-namespace wholesale to match its source paper (Oizumi et al. 2014, Albantakis et al. 2023, Mayner et al. 2026 respectively).
