Macro coarse-grained TPM construction (``pyphi.macro.tpm.macro_tpms``) now hoists three partition-/direction-invariant computations out of its per-unit loop: the system-state weight table (``_state_weights``), the initial-distribution index vectors (``_initial_distributions``), and the patron-unit map (``_patron_units``). These were rebuilt once per macro unit (and per cause/effect direction); they are now built once per call. Coarse-grained cause/effect TPMs are byte-identical.
