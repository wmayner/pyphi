``CompositeMeasure`` carries two new structural attributes that drive IIT 4.0 system-level dispatch: ``applies_ii_cap`` (True for ``INTRINSIC_INFORMATION``, gating the Eq. 23 ``min(φ_c, φ_e, ii(s))`` cap) and ``partition_measure`` (the composite used to score partitions when this measure is the system measure; ``None`` means "use self"). The ``composite_measures.register`` decorator accepts ``applies_ii_cap`` and ``partition_measure`` keyword arguments. Internal dispatch in ``pyphi.formalism.iit4`` and ``pyphi.core.repertoire_algebra`` keys off these attributes (and ``satisfies_composite_measure`` for shape detection) rather than comparing ``measure.name`` to literal strings.
